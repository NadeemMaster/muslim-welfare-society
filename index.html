About Us

Serving The Community Through Better Education & Awareness

The Muslim Welfare Society (MWS) was established on October 1, 1991. For over three decades, we have been dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged communities in Tehsil Liaquat Pur, District Rahim Yar Khan.

Our initiatives address pressing needs in education, health, economic empowerment, and social welfare. Through the collective efforts of our life members, volunteers, and the active participation of the local community, we are laying a foundation for a brighter, more inclusive future for all.

Our Mission

To serve the community through better education, awareness, and welfare programs that uplift the underprivileged and create sustainable social change across Tehsil Liaquat Pur.

Our Vision

A society where every individual, regardless of their background, has access to quality education, economic opportunities, and a dignified life, fostering harmony across all communities.

Our Values

Transparency, inclusivity, compassion, and community empowerment guide every initiative we undertake, ensuring sustainable impact for generations to come.

Our Programs

Changing Lives Through Impactful Programs

We run multiple programs addressing education, welfare, economic empowerment, and environmental sustainability.

Education

Basic Education Community Schools

Established and supervising 34 BECS in underserved areas, providing free education focusing on literacy, character development, and practical skills.

Education

Free Books & Notebooks

Distributed free books and notebooks to 1,680 deserving students from financially disadvantaged backgrounds, enabling them to continue their education.

Women Welfare

Jahaz (Dowry) Fund

Supported over 200 brides with financial assistance for dowries, promoting gender equality and supporting underprivileged families during weddings.

Empowerment

Auto Rickshaw Scheme

Empowered 27 individuals by providing auto rickshaws as a means of livelihood, allowing them to start their own businesses and support their families.

Our Journey of Making a Difference

From a small community initiative to a leading welfare organization — every milestone represents lives touched.

1991

Foundation

Muslim Welfare Society was founded with a vision to serve the underprivileged community of Tehsil Liaquat Pur through education and welfare.

Education

34 BECS Schools Established

Established and supervised 34 Basic Education Community Schools providing free education in underserved areas of the Tehsil.

Future

Technical Institute (Planned)

Youth Sports

Nurturing Youth Through Sports & Discipline

MWS organizes annual sports tournaments for youth across the entire Tehsil. These tournaments involve players from all corners of Liaquat Pur, fostering physical health, teamwork, discipline, and community spirit.

  • Annual Tehsil-wide tournaments
  • Cricket, football & other sports
  • Youth from all communities participate
  • Promotes teamwork & discipline
